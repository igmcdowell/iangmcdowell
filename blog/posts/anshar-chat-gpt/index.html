I Asked ChatGPT To Write My Book Synopsis

December 3, 2022

I’ve been reading a lot about ChatGPT, the latest large language model to come out of OpenAI. I just spent the last year painstakingly writing a technothriller, and wanted to know how close I was to obsolete. I decided to kick the tires and ran ChatGPT through some of the tasks I went through making The Anshar Gambit.

It’s not ready to write a book yet, but I thought the output was pretty damned impressive. I’ve posted the annotated transcript below.

Note: If you haven’t read the book, there are significant spoilers in the chat transcript.

Synopsis Writing#

As she races against time to stop the impending attack, Alice must confront the dark secrets of her company and the people she trusts. Will she be able to prevent disaster, or will she become another victim of the ruthless pursuit of profit?

It’s competent, but I don’t love it. Possibly I’m just biased? The second sentence doesn’t feel adequately set up, and the whole thing reads a little jargon-y. It also makes some erroneous plot assumptions, but nothing unreasonable based on the prompt I provided.

Closing Thoughts#

The technology on display here is mind-blowing. The generated blurbs were just “okay,” but the one I wrote isn’t much better, and I slaved on the damn thing for hours. ChatGPT took all of two seconds. The results here weren’t a huge surprise, summarization and writing marketing copy have long been GPT suggested use-cases.

I was more impressed by the performance on generative tasks. ChatGPT understands basic plot structure, and would get concrete if pressed. It struggled the hardest with planning a scene, but again, it wasn’t terrible, just not particularly good.

It’s at a point now where it could plot and outline a book, albeit with some hand-holding. That doesn’t mean it’d be a particularly good book, but the suggestions it gave were internally consistent and not bat-shit insane, which is an improvement over other models I’ve poked at. I’ll be interested to see how much improvement can be made here.
