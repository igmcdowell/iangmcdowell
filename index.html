The Books

The Boreas Gap

The sky is falling, and it’s Alice’s fault.

Alice runs a private space agency, and today is the highlight of her career. That is, until a meteor strike obliterates London. Someone has turned Alice’s asteroid mining system into a city-killing weapon, and only she knows how to stop it.

Locked out by her boss, Alice must recover a stolen transmitter to hack her way back in. Unfortunately, it’s thousands of miles away, in the heart of a fascist dictatorship. The private army hunting her down isn’t helping either.

Alice is running out of time before the weapon fires again. And the next target… is her.

The Anshar Gambit is a globe-spanning sci-fi thriller set in a near-future dystopia. Come for the badass heroine, stay for the hacking, robots, and explosions.
